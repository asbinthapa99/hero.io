About Me

Hey, I'm Asbin Thapa from London 👋. Photography isn't my job it's just something I love doing in my free time. Whether it's wandering through the city, stumbling on cool architecture, or catching a quiet nature moment, I like freezing those little pieces of life in photos.

For me, photography is less about being perfect and more about having fun, experimenting, and keeping memories. This space is simply where I share those snapshots.
